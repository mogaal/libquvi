
Title: Help

You can help with the quvi project in a number of ways.

Report bugs:

Visit our <Trac at http://sourceforge.net/apps/trac/quvi>. Check for
duplicates before you submit, the issue may have already been fixed.

Write fixes:

Visit our <Trac at http://sourceforge.net/apps/trac/quvi/report/3> for
open tickets. Read also <Help: Style> and <Help: Patches>.

Write support for additional websites:

Visit our <Trac at http://sourceforge.net/apps/trac/quvi/report/3> for
open tickets. See also <Webscript>.

Gather data:

Traffic logs, etc. See also <Webscript: Tutorial> for <Generating network
traffic logs>.

Check the development status:

Visit the <page at
http://sourceforge.net/apps/trac/quvi/wiki/DevelopmentStatus>.
